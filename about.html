About Sunshine Polish & Plating

Since our establishment, Sunshine Polish & Plating has been at the forefront of metal finishing excellence in Hialeah, Florida. Our state-of-the-art facility at 4149 E 10th Ct combines traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver exceptional metal finishing solutions for both industrial and artistic applications.

Our comprehensive range of services includes:

  • Black oxide, nickel, and decorative chrome plating
  • Copper and brass finishing with custom patinas
  • Mirror-finish polishing for all metals
  • Specialized auto trim restoration
  • Custom artistic metal finishing

Our team of skilled craftsmen brings decades of combined experience to every project, ensuring precise attention to detail and superior quality results. Whether you're an automotive enthusiast seeking perfect chrome trim, an artist requiring specialized metal finishes, or an industrial client needing reliable plating services, we have the expertise to exceed your expectations.

We take pride in our ability to handle projects of any scale, from individual artistic pieces to large industrial orders. Our commitment to quality is matched by our dedication to customer service, ensuring that each client receives personalized attention and solutions tailored to their specific needs.
