What we Do

Sunshine Polish & Plating Inc. is a leading metal finishing company in Hialeah, FL, specializing in a comprehensive range of metal finishing services. With our state-of-the-art facility at 4149 E 10th Ct, we deliver exceptional results in black oxide, nickel, decorative chrome, copper, brass, and antique finishes.

Whether you need matte, brushed, or polished finishes, our skilled craftsmen ensure superior quality for both industrial applications and artistic projects.
